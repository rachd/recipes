Banana Bread
Makes One Loaf

Ingredients

3 over-ripe bananas
2 tablespoons butter
2 eggs, beaten until light
2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup chopped coarsely walnuts
Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together bananas, butter, and eggs
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg.
Stir the dry ingredients and the walnuts into the wet ingredients.
Pour the mixture into a buttered 9x5 loaf pan.
Bake for 1 hour.
Remove the loaf from the pan and allow it to cool on its side before cutting.
